Prompt kepada Agent – Standardisasi UI & Logik Master Salary Configuration
Objektif:
Pastikan semua modul gaji (MySyarikat, HRSystem, dan modul lain) guna layout, susunan, label, dan logik pengiraan yang konsisten, tanpa bercampur-campur pattern.

1. Layout & Struktur Tetap
Susunan column (desktop view):

Basic Earning (kiri)

Additional Item (tengah)

Deduction Item (kanan)

Company Contribution (panel biru di kanan / bawah jika mobile)

Basic Earning Section:

Salary Type (dropdown: Monthly / Hourly / Daily)

Basic Salary (RM/Month)

Computed Salary (RM) – read-only, auto update ikut jenis salary type.

Settings (switch/toggle):

Calculated in Payment

SOCSO Enabled

EIS Enabled

EPF Setting:

EPF Calculation Method (dropdown: By Percentage / Fixed)

EPF Employee Rate (%)

EPF Employer Rate (%)

Additional Item Section:

Advance Salary

Subsistence Allowance

Extra Responsibility Allowance

BIK/VOLA (checkbox “Special” + info)

Button View Item/Tax Exemption

Deduction Item Section:

EPF Employee (auto calculated, read-only)

SOCSO Employee (auto calculated, read-only)

EIS Employee (auto calculated, read-only)

Advance Deduction

Unpaid Leave (auto calculated ikut hari)

PCB39 (modal calculation + relief dropdown ikut config standard PCB39)

PCB38

Zakat

Other Deduction

Company Contribution Section:

EPF Employer

SOCSO Employer

EIS Employer

HRDF Employer

Medical Card

Group Term Life

Medical Company

2. Logik Pengiraan Tetap
EPF: (Basic Salary + Allowance yang contribute EPF) × Rate (%)

SOCSO: Guna jadual rasmi (Category 1/2) – auto detect ikut status pekerja.

EIS: 0.2% × Basic Salary (capped RM5,000) – auto detect.

Unpaid Leave: (Basic Salary ÷ Total Working Days in Month) × No. of Leave Days

PCB39:

Relief list ikut config tunggal (rujuk PCB39_RELIEFS_2025).

Auto deduct dari taxable sebelum kira PCB.

Company Contribution ikut statutory settings.

3. Reka Bentuk & UX
Warna & Spacing ikut tema MySyarikat (label di atas input, helper text di bawah input).

Semua “auto calculated” ada teks kecil “How did we calculate this? Based on…”

Semua nilai auto calculated = read-only input (tidak boleh edit manual).

Gunakan ikon ⚙ untuk buka setting tambahan bagi item yang configurable.

4. Kod & Integration
Semua page yang ada fungsi gaji mesti import satu komponen standard (contoh: SalaryConfigForm.vue) supaya UI & logik seragam.

PCB39 modal ambil relief list dari config tunggal.

Pastikan semua pengiraan share utils function tunggal (salaryCalculations.ts) untuk EPF, SOCSO, EIS, PCB39.

5. Acceptance Criteria
Semua page payroll/gaji guna layout, label, dan logik yang sama.

Dropdown PCB39 sama di semua tempat.

Tiada lagi perbezaan istilah atau kedudukan field antara modul.
