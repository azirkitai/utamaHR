Standardize keseluruhan Master Salary Configuration supaya konsisten pada semua page dan ikut logik pengiraan yang betul. Pastikan layout dibahagi kepada 4 kolum tetap:

Basic Earning (warna hijau) – termasuk Salary Type, Basic Salary (RM/Month), Computed Salary (auto), dan semua setting (Calculated in Payment, SOCSO Enabled, EIS Enabled), EPF setting (Calculation Method, Employee Rate, Employer Rate).

Additional Item (warna biru) – Advance Salary, Subsistence Allowance, Extra Responsibility Allowance, BIK/VOLA (Special), dan butang “View Item/Tax Exemption”.

Deduction Item (warna merah) – semua potongan statutory dan lain-lain: EPF Employee, SOCSO Employee, EIS Employee, Advance Deduction, Unpaid Leave, PCB39, PCB38, Zakat, Other Deduction.

Pastikan setiap field ada tooltips: "How did we calculate this? Based on …" untuk jelaskan sumber kiraan.

Company Contribution (warna ungu) – EPF Employer, SOCSO Employer, EIS Employer, HRDF Employer, Medical Card, Group Term Life, Medical Company.

Semua dikira automatik ikut rate semasa × basic salary.

Logik Pengiraan:

Gross Salary = Basic Salary + semua Additional Item.

Total Deduction = jumlah semua nilai dalam Deduction Item.

Net Salary = Gross Salary – Total Deduction.

Company Contribution Total = jumlah semua nilai dalam Company Contribution.

EPF Employee/Employer = Basic Salary × Rate (%) yang ditetapkan.

SOCSO Employee/Employer = ikut jadual rasmi SOCSO berdasarkan Basic Salary.

EIS Employee/Employer = 0.2% × Basic Salary (cap RM5,000).

HRDF Employer = Basic Salary × HRDF Rate.

Advance Deduction = nilai manual yang diisi admin.

Unpaid Leave = (Basic Salary ÷ Working Days) × Bilangan Cuti.

PCB, Zakat, Other Deduction = nilai manual yang diisi admin.

Syarat Paparan & UI:

Semua label nilai dalam RM format (RM X.XX).

Semua jumlah automatik update bila admin tukar rate atau basic salary.

Susunan layout, warna, dan padding konsisten di semua bahagian.

Pastikan butang Calculate Payroll di bawah memaparkan ringkasan: Gross Salary, Total Deduction, Net Salary, dan Company Contribution.
